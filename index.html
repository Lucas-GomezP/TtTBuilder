How to use TtT Builder

TtT Builder is a system designed to create competitive TemTem teams, facilitating both team creation and analysis.

To get started, you'll see 8 slots, each corresponding to a team member, which are initially empty so you can build the team to your liking.

When you select one of these slots, the editor will open up, allowing you to choose your TemTem for that team space. Simply click on the top section of this area.

Clicking on that area will open the TemTems search, where we must choose the one we want to add to our team.

Additionally, we can filter by name to find it more easily.

By clicking on the desired TemTem, it will be automatically loaded into the slot along with its relevant information.

Now, it's time to fill in the other fields. Let's start with the attacks. Clicking on any of the 4 "Techniques" fields will open the search again, displaying the available attacks for that TemTem. Here too, we can type to find the desired attack more easily.

We do the same with the Trait.

And with the gear.

Next, we need to set the TV (Training Values). These have a total limit of 1000 and a maximum of 500 per stat. The system is prepared to control this and automatically update the final stat result.

If you just want to try out the system and see how it works, you can click on the "Load example team" button located in the upper right corner to load a complete team with all its information.

Once you have your complete team, the "Team Summary" section will be enabled, where you can see a summary of your team and its information. At the top, you can click on each team member to disable them from the summary and get a better view of the strengths and weaknesses of each composition.
